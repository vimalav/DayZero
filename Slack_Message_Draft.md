**📊 User Research Insights: watsonx Orchestrate Onboarding**

We completed 3 user interviews with Client Engineering and Account Technical Leaders. Here's what matters most:

**🔴 #1 Critical Finding:**
**Integration/connections to external systems is the biggest blocker** preventing users from building agents. AI engineers often lack credentials to create connections (ServiceNow, Salesforce, Milvus, etc.), leaving them completely stuck.

> "The AI engineer typically does not have the credentials to connect to the back end systems... they're stuck. They can't do anything right." - Joseph Kozhaya

Replace "chat with docs" and "invoice extractor" examples with **Client Zero patterns** (Ask HR, Ask IT, Ask Procurement). Users consistently said these resonate better and showcase our agentic capabilities.

> "A lot of clients resonate with the client 0 strategy... they see a demo and ask procurement, ask HR and they kind of get it." - Cameron Seitz

**📈 Impact:**

- Current time to value: 10-30 minutes
- Target with changes: 5-10 minutes
- MVP improvements already reduce this to 10-15 minutes ✅

## 💡 Tips for Your Slack Message

**Do:**

- Lead with the most actionable insight
- Include a powerful user quote
- Show effort vs impact clearly
- Link to full reports for details
- Make it scannable (use emojis, bold, bullets)

**Don't:**

- Overwhelm with all 10 themes
- Use too much jargon
- Make it too long (people won't read)
- Forget to include next steps/decision needed

**Recommended Length:** 150-250 words for main message, with "Read more" link
