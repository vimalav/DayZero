Quick Start Guide

1

Choose a Client Zero template

Start with Ask HR, Ask IT, or Ask Procurement - proven patterns that work immediately and showcase agentic AI capabilities

2

Set up connections (if needed)

Work with your admin to connect enterprise systems like ServiceNow, Salesforce, or SAP. Many templates work without connections initially.

3

Configure & test

Customize instructions and guardrails, then test with sample queries. The AI assistant can explain configuration options.

4

Deploy & monitor

Launch your agent and track performance metrics, error rates, user feedback, and policy compliance in real-time
