## Design Decisions Documented

### 1. "Needs Attention" Stays Separate from Checklist

**Decision:** Keep "Needs Attention" as a separate section in the center content area

**Rationale:**

- Different mental models (urgent blockers vs. sequential steps)
- Different priorities (reactive vs. proactive)
- Different lifecycles (dynamic vs. static)
- Better visual hierarchy (red banner vs. neutral checklist)

**See:** [`Design_Rationale_v4.0.md`](Design_Rationale_v4.0.md) - Section 3

---

### 2. Checklist in Side Panel (Not Integrated)

**Decision:** Keep checklist in persistent left sidebar (400px width)

**Rationale:**

- Always visible progress tracking
- Persistent context while working
- Clear separation: checklist = map, main area = workspace
- Proven Intercom pattern
- Supports progressive disclosure

**See:** [`Design_Rationale_v4.0.md`](Design_Rationale_v4.0.md) - Section 4

---

### 3. Complete Persona Journeys Documented

**Decision:** Document full user journey for all three personas

**Completed:**

- ✅ Builder Journey: "Build Your First Agent" (≤5 min goal)
- ✅ Admin Journey: "Set Up the Platform" (≤1 hour goal)
- ✅ SME Journey: "Create Your First Workflow" (≤10 min goal)

**Each includes:**

- 5-step checklist
- Day 0 "Needs Attention" items
- Complete journey flow
- Success metrics
- Assistant tab content for all 4 tabs

**See:** [`Design_Rationale_v4.0.md`](Design_Rationale_v4.0.md) - Persona Journeys section

## Testing Recommendations

### A/B Testing:

1. **Test A:** Current v4.0 (separate "Needs Attention")
2. **Test B:** Integrated "Needs Attention" as checklist prerequisites

**Hypothesis:** Separate section will have higher blocker resolution rate

**Metrics:**

- Time to resolve blockers
- Checklist completion rate
- User satisfaction scores
- Support ticket volume

### User Testing:

1. **Recruit:** 3-5 users per persona (9-15 total)
2. **Tasks:**
   - Complete questionnaire
   - Select persona
   - Complete first 2 checklist steps
   - Resolve 1 "Needs Attention" item
   - Use watsonx Assistant for help

3. **Observe:**
   - Where do users get stuck?
   - Do they understand "Needs Attention"?
   - Do they use the Assistant?
   - Do they complete the checklist?

4. **Measure:**
   - Time to first agent
   - Number of errors
   - Support requests
   - Satisfaction scores
